Standalone CV tailoring

Paste the job. Get the tailored CV.

This `cvstudio.work` spinout keeps the strongest part of the original product: upload your base CV once, paste any job description, and generate an ATS-safe tailored version you can review, edit, and export.

What’s included

Copied from the current product

The existing Supabase auth flow, CV upload path, OCR bootstrap, tailoring endpoints, preview renderer, export actions, and ATS keyword inspector.

Intentionally left out

Jobs queue, plans, dashboard, and auto-apply are removed from the main flow so the product stays focused on one job description at a time.
